Eco-life: Pollution

Here are some facts about pollution.

  • Air Pollution

    According to the WHO, about 90% of people worldwide breathe polluted air.

  • Water Pollution

    About 80% of human wastewater is throw into rivers.

  • Soil Pollution

    It's caused primarily by the use of pesticides.

Every person's contribution counts towards bringing these percentages down!
